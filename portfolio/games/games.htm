What is this "After Dark" thing?

All the games that I make, are published under the "After Dark" name.


You can click on this one!

I make use of this logo that is meant to represent light coming off of a CRT television:

The original name that I used was "Far Out games", but that one didn't last for very long as I kept calling it "Far Off Games", also it couldn't be abreviated that effectively.

That's when, during my screensaver fixation, I came across the After Dark Screensavers and really liked the name. Thus After Dark Games was created.
